CS329A Self-Improving AI Agents

Course Overview

This course covers the latest techniques and applications of AI agents that can continuously improve themselves through interaction with themselves and the environment. Key topics include advanced search and retrieval methods like RAG, multi-step reasoning and planning, orchestrating AI capabilities with tools such as LangChain, multimodal web interaction (such as GAIA benchmark), alignment methods such as RLHF and Constitutional AI, data collection and evaluation frameworks, and industry applications spanning code optimization, financial analysis, robotics and more. Our goal is that the students gain deep insights on building AI agents that take actions and use tools to solve interesting and challenging real-world problems.
